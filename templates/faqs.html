Speechsnap is an innovative platform that generates descriptive captions for your images using advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies. Our goal is to help you tell compelling stories through your visual content effortlessly.

Speechsnap utilizes cutting-edge algorithms to analyze the content and context of your images, then generates captions that encapsulate their essence. Simply upload your images, and within moments, you'll have captivating captions ready to accompany them.

Yes, Speechsnap offers a free basic plan that allows users to generate captions for a limited number of images per month. We also offer premium plans with additional features and higher usage limits for those who require more captioning capabilities.

The speak button feature in Speech-Snapallows users to listen to the generated captions being read aloud. Simply click on the speak button next to the caption, and the system will play an audio version of the caption.
